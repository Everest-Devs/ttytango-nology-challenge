A little bit about me!


Before moving into Web development, I've lived many lives.

Up until a few years ago I was studying towards a PhD in Political Science, having previously worked in fields ranging from marketing to human rights.

Prior to learning programming languages, I had become fluent in French, and both spoke and read Myanmar/Burmese language and Mandarin Chinese to a good standard. After a brief delve into R, and then Python I found myself teaching myself the Django web framework, which is where this chapter of my life begins.
